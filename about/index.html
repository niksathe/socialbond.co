What Is Exclusive
Advertising?

Exclusive Advertising is an opportunity to harness the power of a celebrity's audience. Influencer marketing is predicted to be a 10 billion-dollar industry by the end of 2020; meaning it's no secret how valuable leveraging a celebrity's audience is. These campaigns are designed to drive 10x the results than typical brand deals, for a fraction of the price. Socialbond is the network that hosts celebrity campaigns similar to how television networks host commercials during a television series, in our case, their number of followers determines the amount of traffic, as to where a television advertisement's traffic is determined by the number of views during that specific airtime!

This method of advertising has proven far more effective and affordable that typical influencer marketing methods used today, due to the strategic design of the campaign. Harnessing the power of a celebrity's influence on social media, Socialbond has been able to package an advertising campaign that does just that, and in a way that anyone can afford and obtain massive exposure for themselves, their brand, or their movement.

Exclusive Advertising campaigns give brands the ability to establish the social presence of a household name. Our campaigns result in traffic that can be retargeted for free seeing the traffic you will receive is in the form of followers! By obtaining followers, this allows clients to continuously retarget this new audience, increasing the chance of conversion, while simultaneously establishing trust through the social proof developed by the increased following which will increase trust in people viewing!
